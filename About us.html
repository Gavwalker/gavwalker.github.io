History of Hemp

Hemp has been utilized to make fabric as early as 8000-7000 B.C. The first use of cannabis for medicinal purposes was recorded as 2700 B.C. by Shen Nung, one of the fathers of Chinese medicine. In 1484 Pope Innocent VIII labels cannabis as an unholy sacrament of the satanic mass and issues a papal ban on cannabis medicines. Most cultures viewed Hemp as a gift, or treasure, from the Divine Sprit, to be used during ceremonials, at which time it was either burned as incense, ingested for deep meditative and heighten awareness, smoked for pleasure, or worn for clothing during these ceremonies. However, Leafly became the world’s largest cannabis information resource, empowering people in legal cannabis markets to learn about the right products for their lifestyle and wellness needs, find those products safely and efficiently, and buy them from licensed and regulated dispensaries.

History of Jam.Can

Created in 2018 Jam.can when co-founder and CEO Arrickesha Jack along with co-founder and COO Tamika Spence decided that Jamaica needed a prope storefront for cannabis related products. Later that year they brought on Kimeshan Nichalos as Marketing manager and Gavin Walker as brand manager and techincl advisor. Jam.can has now grown to be Jamaica's leading web store for cannabis based products. Providing the highest quality products to local and overseas markets. Jam.can is also a fervent support of the legal cannabis industry in Jamaica and as such have aided several cannabis farmers to become legal exporters of the crop.
